...As a Professional

My career as a Computer Scientist began in 2013, when I started making mods for the PC version of the game 'Skyrim'. This gave me my first experience with modelling tools, scripting, game design, etc. I eventually made many mods that I published onto the Steam Workshop for public download, and they now have more than 100,000 combined downloads. I had a lot of fun making these mods, from learning the technologies needed to fixing flaws in a game I love, so when I saw people care about them and become popular, I loved these little personal projects, especially learning new technologies and fixing flaws in my favorite game. This experience with improving software taught me that this was what I wanted to do with my life. Find ways to give people better experiences with their software, be it a game or a website or anything else. There is something immensely satisfying to me about identifying a problem, and fixing it to make a product better.
